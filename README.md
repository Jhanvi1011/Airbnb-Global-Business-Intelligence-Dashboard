# Airbnb-Global-Business-Intelligence-Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Airbnb Performance Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**. The dashboard provides insights into Airbnb listings, hosts, pricing, reviews, ratings, market share, seasonality, and trust metrics across major global cities.

The objective of this project is to analyze Airbnb’s growth, performance trends, and customer behavior using interactive visualizations and advanced Power BI features.

## 📈 Dashboard Sections & Insights

### 1️⃣ Growth Analysis (2008–2020)

- Airbnb experienced strong growth until **2015 (peak new listings)**.
- 2016–2017 saw restrained growth due to tightening regulations.
- Profitability began in the second half of 2016.
- Growth resumed in 2018 before being impacted by **COVID-19 in 2019–2020**.
- Analysis includes:
  - Total listings by year
  - Property type distribution (Entire Place, Hotel Room, Private Room, Shared Room)

---

### 2️⃣ Market Share by City

- **Paris, New York, and Sydney** account for nearly half of total listings.
- Paris leads in both listings and reviews.
- Contributing factor: hotel prices are nearly double compared to Airbnb in Paris.

---

### 3️⃣ Pricing Analysis

Average price comparison across property types:

- Hotel Room – Highest average price  
- Entire Place  
- Shared Room  
- Private Room  

Interactive selection allows users to drill down by:
- City
- Property type
- Detail level

---

### 4️⃣ Ratings & Detailed Review Metrics

Cities evaluated across:

- Accuracy  
- Cleanliness  
- Communication  
- Location  
- Value  

#### Key Findings:
- Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro are among the best-rated cities.
- Hong Kong and Istanbul score comparatively lower.
- Cleanliness and value-for-money generally score the lowest across cities.

---

### 5️⃣ Review Frequency & Customer Behavior

- 98.8% of customers wrote reviews **3 times or fewer**.
- Majority of customers reviewed only once.
- One extreme case: A user wrote 283 reviews (possible anomaly).

This section helps understand:
- Customer engagement
- Repeat usage behavior
- Data anomalies

---

### 6️⃣ Seasonality Trends

- Paris and Rome dominate reviews from **April to August** (European summer peak).
- New York shows increased review activity in **November and December** (holiday season).
- Monthly trend visualization enables seasonal pattern detection.

---

### 7️⃣ Host Trust & Verification Analysis

- Over **66% of hosts are fully identity verified**.
- Nearly all hosts provide at least one trust signal.
- Very low percentage of anonymous/unverified hosts.

Trust indicators analyzed:
- Profile picture availability
- Identity verification status
